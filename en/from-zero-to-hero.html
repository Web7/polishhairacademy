    WHO IS IT FOR?

    This course is designed for those who are just starting off and are trying to get a taste of the Barbering world and who are ready to leave PHA with confidence. Do you wish for drastic changes? Looking for a way to escape the real world and become an artist? Join an atmosphere of freedom? Then this is the perfect course for you.

    Learn from the best, give it your all and work on your own accords. The art of Barbering in 24 Days is a course that opens a pathway to a new, creative profession that will give you both passion and money in your pocket. Sounds good doesn’t it?

    Not only will you learn the technical aspects, you will learn the finesse that has aided the many greats in the barbering world and allow for an opportunity to properly open your eyes and see the art of cutting, trimming and shaving.

    You will learn the basics and the bases of all hairstyles which are the key of any professional in the business, with this ability you will be able to provide any customer with a solid haircut that is not only interesting but matching to their style or personality.

    We’ve organised the course so that you get as much practice on live models as you possibly can to ensure you develop your skills successfully and have experience with real hair and real heads. With this comes the theory, which will be discussed at PHA with the pros there.

    WHAT ARE YOU LEARNING AND GAINING?

    After the course we hire the best participants to join our pha family or help you in finding the right workplace for you. With our certificate anyone who has knowledge of barbering will be happy to hire you.

    After this course you will be primed and ready to go and you’ll be wanting to cut hair as soon as you enter the bus on the way back home.

    COURSE INCLUDES:

    • Royal shave
    • Razor-blade practices
    • Styling
    • Unique techniques
    • Psychological work with customers
    • Use of social media and promotion of yourself online
    • Geometrical aspects of haircuts

    After the course, we employ the best in our barbershop or help find a job on the market. Everyone who knows about this industry will take you with our certificate.

    Want to know more? Message us or call we will happily answer all your questions.

    Equipment:

    No need to buy equipment for your time at PHA. We provide everything. Have the chance to work with the best brands (Wahl, Andis) to really experience the best and for you to figure out what equipment is perfect for you. After the course all participants have the ability to purchase the equipment used in the course or order the equipment with an exclusive PHA discount.
