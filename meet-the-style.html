    WHO IS IT FOR?

    For the barbers who have short term experiences with Barbering usually 1-2 years. Are you hungry for more knowledge? Are your fascinated by the art of barbering and are willing to learn the advanced tactics and methods used by the masters? Well, that’s great! These courses from the Meet The Style series are elastic. If you require the knowledge of a specific style, choose the 1-Day course. If you want to load your brain with new knowledge of the various modern and popular styles and practices then choose the 3-Day course.

    We’ll share our secrets in trends, we’ll teach you hairstyles that are considered popular and unique by both customers and barbers from the year 2020. The Crop and Pompadour won’t hide any secrets from you.

    You’ll learn the real meaning of the Fade and how to join him with a killer haircut that’ll definitely look CLEAN and FRESH.

    We’ll flood your brain with all the skills and valuable information, like theoretical tricks which you will execute as soon as you step through the door on one of our models.

    WHAT ARE YOU LEARNING AND GAINING?

    You’ll realise that you can do anything, as the newly learnt hairstyles from our course that you propose to your customers will make them become regulars at your shops.

    Day 1. Crop + Fade and Quiff + Fade

    Day 2. Pompadour + Fade and Side-part + Fade

    Day 3. Beard trimming, razor blade practices, beard colouring, turkish techniques, wax techniques, tanning techniques, flossing techniques

    The Course-Leader will explain step by step which elements are key and will present you with a perfect example for both inspiration and to work around.

    You can then grab the scissors and get going, practice is key so repetition will be common so that the knowledge you receive will be embedded in your head just like your date of birth or your phone number. The course leader is always with you to answer questions and there to show you the various tips and tricks for you to reach the final effect.

    Equipment Required:

    Main Clipper with guards, trimmer, shaver, scissors, thinning scissors, hair-dryer, spray-bottle, razor-blade for contouring.
